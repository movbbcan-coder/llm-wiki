# Vaswani et al.

## Overview

Research team that published the seminal paper **"Attention Is All You Need"** in 2017, introducing the [[Transformer]] architecture.

## Key Contributions

- Introduced the [[Transformer]] architecture
- Demonstrated that attention mechanisms alone (without recurrence) can achieve state-of-the-art results
- Pioneered [[Self-Attention]] and [[Multi-Head Attention]] techniques
